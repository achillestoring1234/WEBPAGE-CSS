San Remigio National High School offers two programs for Junior High School, one of which is the Basic Education Program. This program focuses on providing fundamental sessions dedicated to produce confident students from grades 7 to 10. Another program offered is the Department of Science and Technology Accredited Program, which is the Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics Education Program. Specially offered for incoming grade 7 students who wish to journey on a ride with Science. With the advent of the K to 12 curriculum, San Remigio National High School quickly adapted to the new curriculum and offers academic track.
